Mr. Eric Mensah

CEO

Eric Nana Mensah popularly known as Dr Real is the CEO of DR REAL ENTERTAINMENT. He is a Ghanaian from Shai Hills in the Greater Accra Region. His vision is to help bring out the best in young and upcoming artistes in the music industry.

His passion for music has been innate since his early days and has decided to channel that energy into nurturing young acts.

His dream as a label owner is to see his artistes yield positive dividends in the industry and become role models for other young people with dreams if being Artistes. he has vowed never to rest until good music and arts is spread all over the universe.
