About Us

Dr Real Entertainment is a record label and artiste management company based in West Africa - Ghana, that seeks to empower emerging artistes by providing them with the necessary arsenals to create and promote audio and visual contents.

The label buys into the dreams of upcoming artistes who have the zeal and talent to show the world what they have. Music is a universal language that unites people of different cultures as well as influence people in various ways hence the focus .
Our vision is to take African Music to global heights and tell stories from the artiste's perspectives.
We have a lot up our sleeves and it's quality contents for our viewers worldwide.

Mr. P. K. Acheampong

Manager

Philip Acheampong is the label manager at Dr Real Entertainment. He's a Guan from the Oti Region of Ghana and very passionate about music and the business surounding it. he has worked with some emerging Artistes in the country and provided creative needs to them as well.
His vision is to create a new wave of artistes who respect the ethnics of music and bring their best to reap egregious results. Music to him is a product. It's functionality and aesthetic must be pleasing to the audience in various regions not just Africa. Dr Real Entertainment is here to make a mark in the Ghanaina music industry, stay tuned.
