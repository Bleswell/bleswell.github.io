Charisma

Afrobeat Artiste

CHARISMA, name in full as Charisma Worlanyo Logo is a singer and a songwriter from the helms of Juapong in the Volta Region. He was brought forth by Elizabeth Adzokpa and Daniel Logo.

The Artiste completed his basic education at Witsand International School and proceeded to St. Martins Senior High for his secondary education. He later delve into media and gained admission to Insite Media College, where he acquired a diploma in broadcast jornalism.

Charisma's interest in the performing arts begun from an early stage that premeditated his entry into City-TV Voice Factory where he was fans favourite as week by week he kept delivering very captivating performances but did not emerge the winner. He however dropped out and released his first single "CONFESS" which sparked a debate as to whether it was a Burna Boy record or not.

Charisma is an Afrobeat/Afrodancehall Artiste and he looks up to the likes of Stonebwoy, Burna Boy and Popcan. A Storm is coming, not one that comes to destroy but one that comes with the vocal prowess to make a statement in the industry.
